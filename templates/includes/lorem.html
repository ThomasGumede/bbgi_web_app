The dashboard allows you to **track ticket orders**, view both **pending and completed transactions**, and handle any necessary order modifications. By monitoring paid orders and pending payments, you can ensure a smooth check-in experience for your guests. With these tools at your fingertips, you can efficiently manage your event from start to finish, keeping everything organized and professional.
